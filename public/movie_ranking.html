The Best Movies According to Prakhar

My top 4 movies of all time.

PK

The movie, a complete package, not only tickles your funny bone but also gives you a new perspective toward life and religions

The Dark Knight Rises

Although, the complete Batman Trilogy from Nolan is amazing, but this one is exceptional. The story, the direction, the action, the dialogues everything is just perfect.

The Interstellar

Another masterpiece by Nolan, this movie makes us realize what the true potential of the sci-fi genre is. Nolan visualized a black hole for this movie even before we had an actual picture and his visualization is very similar to the actual image. All hail Nolan's genius.
